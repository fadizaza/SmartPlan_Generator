!!! Slide 1: What is an AI Agent?

*   An AI agent is a computer program that acts in an environment.
*   It perceives its environment and takes actions to achieve goals.
*   Think of it like a robot or a virtual assistant!
*   We'll look at different types of AI agents.


!!! Slide 2: Reactive AI Agents

*   These agents react directly to their environment.
*   No memory of past experiences.
*   Example: Deep Blue (chess-playing AI).
*   Simple, but limited in capabilities.


!!! Slide 3: Memory-Based AI Agents

*   These agents *do* remember past experiences.
*   They use this memory to make decisions.
*   Example: A self-driving car learning from past routes.
*   More adaptable than reactive agents.


!!! Slide 4: Goal-Oriented AI Agents

*   These agents have specific goals to achieve.
*   They plan and make decisions to reach those goals.
*   Example: A robot vacuum cleaner cleaning a room.
*   Most complex type of AI agent.


!!! Slide 5: AI Agents in the Real World

*   Self-driving cars (Transportation)
*   Spam filters (Communication)
*   Medical diagnosis tools (Healthcare)
*   Recommendation systems (E-commerce)


!!! Slide 6: Ethical Considerations

*   Bias in algorithms can lead to unfair outcomes.
*   Job displacement due to automation.
*   Privacy concerns with data collection.
*   Accountability for AI agent actions.


!!! Slide 7: Case Study:  Robot Vacuum

*   Goal: Clean a room efficiently.
*   Sensors: Detect dirt, obstacles.
*   Actions: Move, clean, recharge.
*   Effectiveness: Measured by cleanliness & battery life.
*   Limitations: Struggles with stairs, complex layouts.
