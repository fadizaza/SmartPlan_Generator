!!! Slide 1: What is an AI Agent?

*   An AI agent is a computer program that acts in an environment.
*   It perceives its environment and takes actions to achieve goals.
*   Think of it like a robot, but it can be software too!
*   We'll look at different types of agents.


!!! Slide 2: Reactive AI Agents

*   These agents react directly to their environment.
*   No memory of past events.
*   Simple, but limited capabilities.
*   Example: A simple chess program that only looks at the current board state.


!!! Slide 3: Memory-Based AI Agents

*   Remember past experiences.
*   Use past experiences to inform current actions.
*   More adaptable than reactive agents.
*   Example: A self-driving car learning from past driving situations.


!!! Slide 4: Goal-Oriented AI Agents

*   Have specific goals to achieve.
*   Plan and make decisions to reach goals.
*   Most complex type of agent.
*   Example: A robot designed to navigate a maze.


!!! Slide 5: Comparing AI Agents

| Agent Type      | Memory | Planning | Example             |

!!! Slide 6: AI Agents in Action!

*   Healthcare: Diagnosing diseases.
*   Finance: Fraud detection.
*   Gaming:  Non-player characters (NPCs).
*   Customer Service: Chatbots.


!!! Slide 7: Ethical Considerations

*   Bias in algorithms.
*   Job displacement.
*   Privacy concerns.
*   Accountability for actions.


!!! Slide 8: Case Study: Spam Filters

*   Goal: Identify and filter spam emails.
*   Agent Type: Memory-based.
*   Effectiveness: High accuracy, reducing unwanted emails.
*   Limitations: Can sometimes flag legitimate emails as spam.
