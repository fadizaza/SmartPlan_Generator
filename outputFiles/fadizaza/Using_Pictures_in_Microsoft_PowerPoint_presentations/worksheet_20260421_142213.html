Using Pictures in Microsoft PowerPoint Presentations

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this worksheet, you will be able to:

  • Insert a picture from your computer into a PowerPoint slide
  • Locate the "Insert" tab in PowerPoint
  • Resize a picture by dragging its corners

Activity: Adding and Resizing a Picture in PowerPoint

  • Open Microsoft PowerPoint.
  • Click "Blank Presentation" to start a new slide.
  • Look at the top menu bar. Find the "Insert" tab (it has icons like a picture, chart, and video).

    • *(Note: If you don't have an image, describe where the "Insert" tab is located.)

  • Click the "Insert" tab.
  • Click the "Pictures" button (it looks like a photo frame).
  • A window will open. Browse your computer for a picture (e.g., a family photo, a favorite animal, or a fun image).
  • Double-click the picture to insert it onto your slide.
  • Look at the small squares (handles) at the corners of the picture.
  • Place your mouse cursor on any corner handle (it will turn into a double-headed arrow).
  • Click and drag the corner to resize the picture (make it bigger or smaller).
  • Try resizing your picture to fit nicely on the slide.
  • Click "File" (top-left corner).
  • Click "Save As".
  • Choose a name (e.g., "My First PowerPoint").
  • Click "Save" to save your presentation.
