!!! Slide 1: What is an AI Agent?

*   An AI agent is a computer program that acts in an environment.
*   It perceives its environment and acts to achieve goals.
*   Think of it like a robot or a smart assistant!
*   Different types of agents have different capabilities.

!!! Slide 2: Reactive AI Agents

*   Respond directly to their environment.
*   No memory of past experiences.
*   Example: Deep Blue (chess-playing AI).
*   Simple but limited.

!!! Slide 3: Memory-Based AI Agents

*   Remember past experiences.
*   Use past experiences to make decisions.
*   Example: Self-driving car learning routes.
*   More adaptable than reactive agents.

!!! Slide 4: Goal-Oriented AI Agents

*   Have specific goals they try to achieve.
*   Plan and strategize to reach goals.
*   Example: Robot vacuum cleaner cleaning a room.
*   Most complex type of agent.
