Tips for Effective Learning Outcomes
  • Be specific and measurable
  • Use action verbs (identify, explain, analyze, create, etc.)
  • Focus on what students will learn or be able to do
  • Align outcomes with curriculum requirements
  • Consider different levels of learning (knowledge, comprehension, application, analysis, etc.)
