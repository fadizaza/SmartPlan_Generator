About the Lesson Generator

What is the Lesson Generator?

The Lesson Generator is an AI-powered application designed to assist educators in creating comprehensive teaching materials. It streamlines the process of developing lesson plans, worksheets, assessments, presentations, and revision materials.

Features

  • Generate learning outcomes from topics or topics from learning outcomes
  • Create detailed lesson plans with customizable sections
  • Design student worksheets and activities
  • Develop various types of assessment questions
  • Build PowerPoint presentations
  • Produce revision materials and study guides

How to Use

Begin by entering a topic and learning outcomes on the home page. Then, navigate to the specific tool you need (lesson plans, worksheets, questions, etc.) and follow the on-screen instructions to generate your teaching materials.
